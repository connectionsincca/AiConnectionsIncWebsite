FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know about getting started with AI for your Canadian business.

Do I need technical knowledge or an IT team to use your solutions?
Absolutely not. We design every solution specifically for business owners and their staff — not developers. Our tools come with intuitive interfaces, plain-language dashboards, and full onboarding training for your team. We handle all the technical complexity behind the scenes so you can focus on running your business. If you can use a smartphone, you can use our AI tools.
How much does an AI solution typically cost for a small business?
Our solutions are priced to be accessible. Depending on complexity, projects typically range from a few thousand to tens of thousands of dollars — a fraction of what enterprise AI firms charge for comparable results. We offer flexible payment options and phased implementations to fit SMB cash flow. Book a free consultation and we'll give you a transparent, itemized estimate with zero surprises.
How quickly can I have an AI solution up and running?
Speed is one of our biggest differentiators. Most of our solutions go live within 2 to 6 weeks from the initial consultation — not months. We use proven frameworks, pre-built components, and an agile delivery process to get you results fast. In most cases you'll see a working prototype within the first week of the build phase.
Is my business data safe and private?
Data privacy is non-negotiable for us. All solutions we build are fully compliant with Canada's PIPEDA regulations and applicable provincial privacy laws. Your data stays yours — we never sell, share, or use your business data to train models. We also offer deployments in Canadian data centres to satisfy data residency requirements where needed.
What if the AI solution doesn't deliver results for my business?
We stand behind our work with a results guarantee. Before any project begins, we define clear, measurable success metrics together. If the solution isn't hitting agreed benchmarks within the first 90 days post-launch, we iterate and optimize at no additional cost until it does. We don't disappear after delivery — your success is our reputation and our business.
Do you provide ongoing support after the solution launches?
Yes — and this is a core part of how we work. We offer monthly support and optimization retainers that keep your AI solution performing at its best as your business evolves. AI systems improve with more data over time, and we actively monitor and tune your solution so it keeps getting better, not just sitting still after go-live.
My business is in a niche industry — can you still help?
Absolutely — niche industries are often where AI creates the most dramatic advantages. We've worked across hospitality, retail, healthcare, professional services, manufacturing, real estate, logistics, and more. Our discovery process is designed to deeply understand your specific workflows and competitive dynamics before we build a single line of code. The more specific your industry, the more targeted we can make your solution.
