§ 05 — FAQ

Questions We Hear Often

Straight answers. No corporate speak.

Do I need technical knowledge or an IT team?
+
Absolutely not. Every solution is designed for business owners and staff — not developers. Intuitive interfaces, plain-language dashboards, full team training included. If you can use a smartphone, you can use our tools.
How much does an AI solution cost for a small business?
+
Pricing is designed for SMB budgets — a fraction of enterprise AI firms. Projects typically range from a few thousand to tens of thousands depending on complexity. Flexible payment options available. Book a free consult for a transparent, itemized estimate with no surprises.
How quickly can I have something live?
+
2–6 weeks from initial consultation in most cases. We use proven frameworks and pre-built components to deliver fast. Most clients see a working prototype in the first week of the build phase.
Is my business data safe and private?
+
Data privacy is non-negotiable. All solutions comply with PIPEDA and applicable provincial privacy laws. Your data stays yours — never sold, shared, or used to train models. Canadian data centres available for data residency requirements.
What if results don't meet expectations?
+
We define clear, measurable success metrics before any project begins. If agreed benchmarks aren't met within 90 days post-launch, we iterate and optimize at no additional cost until they are. We don't disappear after delivery.
Do you provide ongoing support after the solution launches?
+
Yes — and this is a core part of how we work. We offer monthly support and optimization retainers that keep your AI solution performing at its best as your business evolves. AI systems improve with more data over time, and we actively monitor and tune your solution so it keeps getting better, not just sitting still after go-live.
My business is in a niche industry — can you still help?
+
Absolutely — niche industries are often where AI creates the most dramatic advantages. We've worked across hospitality, retail, healthcare, professional services, manufacturing, real estate, logistics, and more. Our discovery process is designed to deeply understand your specific workflows and competitive dynamics before we build a single line of code. The more specific your industry, the more targeted we can make your solution.
